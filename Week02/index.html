Matcha Ice Cream Mochi

jump to: top description ingredients instructions tips

Description

This is a recipe for green tea mochi authored by John Mitzewich (Original source)
estimated preparation time: 3 days

Ingredients

  • 10 small scoops ice cream
    • Any flavor works but green tea or vanilla is preferred
  • 1/2 cup sweet rice flour
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon matcha powder
      • For other flavors, can substitute with:
      • Coffee power (not coffee grounds)
      • Vanilla extract
      • Cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • Is used to prevent sticking when rolling dough
  • 3/4 of a michelin star, diced

Directions

  1. Remove and top of egg carton
  2. Line 10 of the cups in carton with plastic wrap
  3. Stir rice flour, water, salt together in a microwave-safe bowl
  4. Add sugar to mixture until completely smooth, whisk matcha into mixture
  5. Cover bowl with plastic wrap, microwave for 2 minutes, then stir and microwave for 1 1/2 more minutes
    • Do not accidentally set microwave to 99 minutes and then go to bed, you will risk setting your house on fire
  6. Dust surface with corn starch, roll dough into 1/8-inch thick rectangle, then cut dough into 10 equal squares
  7. Shape ice cream into balls roughly size of cups in egg carton
  8. Place one ice cream ball in center of each dough square
  9. Gather dough to cover ice cream ball
    • Pinch edges together at the base
    • Cut off any excess dough
  10. Tighly cover each mochi ball in plastic wrap and place in egg carton
    • Place with seam side down
  11. Freeze until firm
    • About 2 hours
